# Release Notes - v3.6

## New Features
- **Mirror Mode**: Added a new standard feature in Display Mode to horizontally flip the video stream, perfect for correcting camera orientation or self-view setups.
- **Improved Audio/Video Sync**: Enhanced the synchronization between audio and video streams for a more natural viewing experience.

## Fixes & Improvements
- **Video Display Regression**: Fixed a critical issue where the remote video stream would fail to appear on the first connection.
- **Reconnection Stability**: Resolved bugs preventing video from resuming correctly after an app restart or disconnection loop.
- **Smoother Connections**: Consolidated internal connection logic to prevent race conditions during the initial handshake.
- **Dependency Updates**: Updated internal libraries for better performance and security.
